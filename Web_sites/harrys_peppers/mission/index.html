Our mission

Our mission is to introduce traditional Peruvian ingredients to Brooklyn restaurants. In addition, we try to do so in a manner that is environmentally sustainable and replicable on a large scale. In doing so, we hope to introduce a form of agriculture that is adapted to urban environments to Brooklyn; and by creating a market for Peruvian ingredients, we hope to improve the market viability of these products for Peruvian farmers so that they won't have to rely on growing coca for cocaine production.
